3rd Person Camera Control

Camera interaction with the environment

This is a 3rd Person Dynamic Camera Control for Unity 5. It interacts with occlusion and colliding with the enviorment of the game world, and moves the camera closer to the player character, depending on the location of the game object witch interferes in between the camera and the player.
